General Assembly WDI Remote - Graduation Nov, 22, 2016

After studying Economics at UCLA, travelling around the world, and working in finance for several years it became clear to me that something was missing. Throughout my youth I demonstrated creativity and passion for technology and programming. Even in college I studied computer science and C++. More recently, while working at the trading desk, I rediscovered this passion and began studying web development in my free time... until I found GA. Then everything changed. I took the leap! The GA WDI course is fast-paced and thorough. It's incredible how much I've learned and accomplished in such a short amount of time. But it's not easy; it requires great dedication. Going forward I hope to join a team that allows me to implement the knowledge and skills I have gained in a creative and innovative environment.
