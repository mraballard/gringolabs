Who me?

After studying Economics at UCLA, I spent several years living and travelling around the world, absorbing foreign cultures and languages. Eventually I found myself working in finance but something was missing. I rediscoverd my love for programming and dove into the General Assembly Web Development Immersive Bootcamp and never looked back!


A passionate web developer and citizen of the world. I bring unconventional, innovative thinking and a global perspective to the team. I am a knowledge seeker, a free thinker, looking to work on something meaningful and driven by the idea of creating something fantastic.
